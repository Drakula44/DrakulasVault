$$
p(\theta|x) = \frac{p(x|\theta) p(\theta)}{p(x)}
$$
gde je $p(x)$:$$
p(x) = \int p(x|\theta)p(\theta)d\theta
$$
Greska: $$
\epsilon = \theta - \hat{\theta}
$$
Greska: $$
C(\epsilon)
$$
Rizik: $$
R = E\{ C(\epsilon) \}
$$
optimalni estimator: $$
\hat{\theta}^{*} = \underset{ \theta }{ argmin } R
$$
